### 🧑🏻‍💻 About Me
Frontend Engineer focused on building architecturally resilient interfaces. I transform complex business requirements into predictable, maintainable, and scalable code.

### 👓 Experience
* Architecture: I design systems prioritizing Feature-Sliced Design (FSD) and clear separation of concerns.
* Quality & Performance: I specialize in stable hydration (SSR/CSR) and optimizing critical rendering paths.
* Core Principles: I treat documentation as a core part of the codebase. I favor architectural clarity over unnecessary complexity.

### 🔭 Goals
* Deepen my expertise in large-scale system design for frontend applications.
* Implement efficient development processes to minimize technical debt in legacy projects.
* Continuous learning and adaptation of emerging frontend technologies.
